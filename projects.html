Custom Wedding Wishlist

Wedding wishlist for guests to mark things they have bought for the couple.

Aims to emulate simliar lists provided by certain web shops, while remaining completely general.

Not everything the couple wishes for can be found at a specific store, or may not be sold in a store at all. This project aims to give the owners a way to publish their wishlist without being limited to a specific store.

The resulting website from this project allows the owners to add wishes to their wishlist, and allows the users/guests to check gifts that has been aquired for the couple. This allows the couple to maintain an overview of gifts that has been aquired, but most importantly helps other guests not aquire the same gifts that already has been aquired.
